BLEACH

Description :

Bleach follows Ichigo Kurosaki, a teenager who becomes a Soul Reaper and battles evil spirits known as Hollows to protect the living world and lost souls.

Genre :

Action, Adventure, Supernatural, Fantasy.

Production Date :

The anime aired from 2004 to 2012, with the Thousand-Year Blood War Arc starting in 2022.

Time :

366 episodes (main series) + additional arcs.

Author

Tite Kubo.
